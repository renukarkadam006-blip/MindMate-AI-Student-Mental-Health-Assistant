MindMate – AI Student Mental Health Assistant

Overview

MindMate is an AI-powered student mental health assistant designed to help students manage stress, anxiety, and academic pressure. The application promotes emotional well-being through mood tracking, wellness activities, AI support, and study productivity tools.

SDG Goal

SDG 3 – Good Health and Well-Being

Features

- Mood Tracker
- AI Wellness Chatbot
- Daily Motivation
- Stress Relief Tools
- Study Focus Timer (Pomodoro)
- Mental Wellness Tips
- Emergency Support Information
- Progress Dashboard

Benefits

- Supports student mental health
- Encourages positive habits
- Improves study focus
- Promotes self-care and well-being

Future Enhancements

- Personalized wellness plans
- Advanced AI counseling support
- Community support groups
- Integration with wearable devices
